Enchiladas

A savory red sauce baked dish

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground Beef
  • Enchilada Sauce
  • Sour Cream
  • Four Cheese Mexican Blend

Directions

  1. Brown Meat in Pan
  2. Drain Meat
  3. Coat Tortillas in Enchilada Sauce
  4. Roll meat and onions into the tortillas
  5. Place each enchilada tightly in the backing dish
  6. Top with remaining cheese and sauce
  7. Bake for 40 minutes at 350 degrees F
