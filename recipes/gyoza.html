Gyoza

Wonton wrapped nuggets of Umami Joy

Ingredients

  • Gyoza/wonton wrappers
  • Ground Pork
  • Green Onion
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil

Directions

  1. Brown Meat in Pan
  2. Drain Meat
  3. Add Green Onion and Sesame Oil
  4. Roll meat and onions into the wrappers
  5. Gently crimp each packet and seal with wet fingers
  6. Pan fry to get a crisp edge
  7. Cover skillet with lid and steam for 5 minutes
