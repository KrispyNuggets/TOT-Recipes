Gyudon

Rice Bowl Home Cooking

Ingredients

  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin
  • Sake
  • Brown Sugar
  • Shabu Shabu cut beef
  • Rice (pre cooked)
  • Cheese
  • Onions

Directions

  1. Brown Meat in Pan
  2. Drain Meat
  3. Add onions
  4. Cook until translucent
  5. Add soy sauce, mirin, sake, and brown sugar
  6. Bring to a simmer for 10 minutes
  7. Serve over rice and enjoy
